>>> Full description (max 4000 chars)
Velugu is a calm, cozy 30-day journey into spoken Telugu — designed for absolute beginners who just want to talk, not memorize the script.

This isn't a quiz machine. It's a small village where every phrase you learn lights a diya.

WHAT YOU'LL DO

• Start with the simplest greetings and build to real conversations
• See every phrase in English first, then in spoken Telugu
• Tap to hear native-sounding audio for any word
• Use your mic to practise saying phrases out loud — get scored on pronunciation
• Play through 5 branching scenario sims (chai stall, market, auto ride, family visit, asking directions)
• Collect diyas as you go. No streaks to fear, no guilt loops.

THE CURRICULUM (30 days, 4 regions)

Week 1 — The Lane You Live On — Greetings, your name, basic numbers
Week 2 — The Morning Market — Family, food, time, weather
Week 3 — Streets & Bus Stops — Directions, shopping, transport, asking for help
Week 4 — The Festival of Lights — Work, hobbies, feelings, real conversations

FOR EARLY LEARNERS

• English-first throughout — the Telugu script is optional
• Romanised pronunciation shown for every phrase
• Pick your guide: Anu (cheerful), Saraswati amma (warm grandmother), or Rohan (casual)
• Mood-aware sessions: pick Slow / Good / Bright on any day
• Daily review surfaces phrases you're about to forget (spaced repetition)
• Weak Spots mode drills the exact phrases you keep missing

ALSO IN HERE

• Phrasebook of 150+ everyday phrases, fully searchable
• Light and dark themes
• Works completely offline after first launch
• No account, no sign-up, no ads, no tracking
• All your progress stays on your device

WHO IT'S FOR

• Anyone curious about Telugu — travelers, partners marrying into Telugu families, NRIs reconnecting, language enthusiasts
• Total beginners who want to speak, not study
• Anyone tired of language apps that punish you for missing a day

WHO IT'S NOT FOR

• Advanced learners — this is a foundation, not a graduate course
• Anyone who wants to read & write the Telugu script first (we don't focus on that)

This is a small, lovingly-built app. If you stick with it for 30 days, you'll genuinely be able to hold a simple Telugu conversation. After that, find a native speaker — that's where real fluency lives.
