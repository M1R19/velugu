Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 May 2026

Velugu is a learning app designed to respect your privacy completely. The short version: nothing leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not run analytics, telemetry, advertising, or any third-party tracking on this app.

What we store locally on your device

To remember your progress between sessions, the app saves the following only on your device using your browser's built-in localStorage:

  • Which lessons you've completed and your quiz scores
  • Your study streak and the date of your last session
  • Your chosen guide character, theme, font size, and other settings
  • Your spaced-repetition schedule (which phrases need review and when)
  • Your selected interests and mood (from onboarding)
  • Which scenarios you've played

This data never leaves your device. We have no servers; there is no account; you don't sign in to anything. Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app deletes all of it permanently.

Microphone

If you use the "Say It" pronunciation-grading feature, the app asks your browser's permission to access your microphone. Your speech is processed by your own browser's built-in speech-recognition engine (the W3C Web Speech API). The audio is not sent to us. Different browsers handle this differently — for instance, Chrome may send audio to Google's speech servers as part of its own service. We have no control over and no access to this data; it is governed by your browser vendor's own privacy policy.

Text-to-speech

When you tap a "Play" button to hear a phrase, the app uses your browser's built-in text-to-speech engine. We do not send the text to any external service. Your operating system may have its own behaviour for synthesising speech; that is outside our control.

Third parties

The app loads three resources from Google's open font CDN (Fonts.googleapis.com and Fonts.gstatic.com): the Fraunces, Inter, and Noto Serif Telugu fonts. Loading a font may expose your IP address to Google according to their own privacy policy. We do not control what Google logs.

The app contains no other third-party scripts, trackers, or pixels.

Children

Velugu is suitable for all ages and collects no information from anyone, including children under 13. It is not directed at children specifically, but contains no content that would be inappropriate for them.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since the app has no notification system, we recommend re-checking this page from time to time if you have concerns.

Contact

For any questions, open an issue at github.com/M1R19/velugu/issues.
